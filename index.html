Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working and chilling at Kopi Ruang.

What are your opening hours?
We are open every day from 07:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Perfect for early morning focus sessions or late-night deadline crunches.

Is there a time limit for using the workspace?
There are no strict time limits. However, during our peak hours (usually 3 PM - 6 PM), we appreciate fair use and might kindly encourage sharing larger tables with other remote workers.

Do you provide prayer room (Mushola) facilities?
Yes, we have a clean and comfortable Mushola located on the 2nd floor, complete with separate ablution (wudhu) areas and prayer garments.

Can I reserve a table or meeting room?
General seating is first-come, first-served. However, we do have a private glass meeting room (fits up to 6 people) that can be booked in advance via WhatsApp or our online ordering portal.

Do you offer non-dairy milk alternatives?
Absolutely! We offer premium Oat Milk and Almond Milk upgrades for any of our espresso-based or signature milk beverages.
